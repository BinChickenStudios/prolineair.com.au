Services

Installation

At Proline Air-Conditioning, we take pride in our personalized approach to air conditioning installations. As a smaller business, we offer attentive and customized services. Our experienced technician will visit your location to carefully assess your needs and recommend the most suitable air conditioning system. We ensure precise installation, including proper sizing, positioning, and all necessary connections, to guarantee your comfort.

Maintenance

Our maintenance services are designed to keep your air conditioning system running smoothly without breaking your budget. We offer flexible maintenance plans tailored to your specific needs. Our technician will conduct regular inspections, clean essential components like filters and coils, and perform routine tasks like checking refrigerant levels and lubricating moving parts to keep your system in top condition.

Repairs

When your air conditioning system encounters issues, Proline Air-Conditioning is here to provide prompt and cost-effective solutions. Our technician will quickly diagnose the problem and perform necessary repairs, always with an eye on cost-effectiveness. We understand the importance of your budget as a smaller business and aim to keep your system running efficiently without unnecessary expenses.

Refrigerant Management

Ensuring safe and efficient handling of refrigerants is one of our priorities. Our technician is well-trained to manage refrigerants, including recharging levels, identifying and addressing leaks, and maintaining environmental and health safety standards.

Ductwork Services

Proline Air-Conditioning's technician pays close attention to the ductwork of your HVAC system. We inspect and maintain ducts, addressing any leaks or damage, which is especially important for a smaller business where every bit of efficiency counts.

Thermostat Installation and Calibration

We handle thermostat installation and calibration with precision. We make sure your thermostat is accurately set to maintain a comfortable indoor temperature while optimizing energy efficiency, which can help you save on operating costs.

Emergency Services

We recognize the value of uninterrupted cooling for your smaller business, especially during emergencies. Proline Air-Conditioning offers responsive 24/7 emergency repair services to ensure you have a reliable cooling solution when you need it most.

System Upgrades and Replacement

If your existing air conditioning system is no longer cost-effective or efficient, our technician will assess your situation and provide recommendations for affordable system upgrades or replacements to improve performance.

Consultations and Assessments

Proline Air-Conditioning offers personalized consultations and assessments, considering your budget constraints. We can perform cooling load calculations and provide tailored recommendations to enhance system efficiency and comfort.
